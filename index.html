Red Roofed Barn

This photo was taken at the Virginia Tech Smithfield horse center. I was fascinated with the accents of red in this photo with the entire barn being white and the door and roof being a solid red. The bright and vibrant color of red really stood out against the solid white. I enjoyed how there was a white VT symbol painted on the door which tied the whole thing together.

About me

Hello! My name is Olivia Merklinger and I am currently a sophomore at Virginia Tech. I am from Virginia Beach, Virginia and have a family of 6. I am the oldest and have two younger sisters and a young brother, as well as a chocolate lab named Bodie. I love to travel, go on hikes, swim in the ocean, and any water activites. I enjoy anything that has to deal with being outdoors which is one of the main reasons I really liked taking photos for this photo gallery.
